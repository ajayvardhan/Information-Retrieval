<p><b>Expedition 17</b> was the 17th expedition to the <a href="/wiki/International_Space_Station" title="International Space Station">International Space Station</a> (ISS). The first two crew members, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sergey_Volkov_(astronaut)" title="Sergey Volkov (astronaut)">Sergey Volkov</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Oleg_Kononenko" title="Oleg Kononenko">Oleg Kononenko</a> were launched on 8 April 2008, aboard the <a href="/wiki/Soyuz_TMA-12" title="Soyuz TMA-12">Soyuz TMA-12</a>. Once aboard the station, they joined <a href="/wiki/Garrett_Reisman" title="Garrett Reisman">Garrett Reisman</a>, who transferred from <a href="/wiki/Expedition_16" title="Expedition 16">Expedition 16</a> to join the Expedition 17 crew.</p>
<p>Reisman was replaced by <a href="/wiki/Gregory_Chamitoff" title="Gregory Chamitoff">Gregory Chamitoff</a>, who launched aboard the <a href="/wiki/Space_Shuttle_Discovery" title="Space Shuttle Discovery">Space Shuttle <i>Discovery</i></a> mission <a href="/wiki/STS-124" title="STS-124">STS-124</a> on 31 May 2008. Volkov and Kononenko landed safely on 24 October 2008, while Chamitoff remained aboard the station as an <a href="/wiki/Expedition_18" title="Expedition 18">Expedition 18</a> crewmember.</p>

<ul>
<li>Sergei Volkov, 35, was the youngest person to command the ISS.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></li>
<li>Garrett Reisman was the first Jewish resident of the ISS.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-first_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-first-4">[4]</a></sup></li>
<li>Gregory Chamitoff was the first crewmember to take bagels with him to the station; three bags of 18 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sesame_seed" title="Sesame seed">sesame seed</a> <a href="/wiki/Montreal-style_bagel" title="Montreal-style bagel">bagels</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></li>
<li>Reisman launched to the station on <a href="/wiki/STS-123" title="STS-123">STS-123</a> in March, which was during the <a href="/wiki/Expedition_16" title="Expedition 16">Expedition 16</a> spaceflight. He landed on <a href="/wiki/STS-124" title="STS-124">STS-124</a>.</li>
<li>Chamitoff launched to the station on STS-124 in June, and stayed through <a href="/wiki/Expedition_18" title="Expedition 18">Expedition 18</a>, leaving the station on <a href="/wiki/STS-126" title="STS-126">STS-126</a> in November.</li>
</ul>
