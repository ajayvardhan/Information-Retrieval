<p><b>Gloria Rodr√≠guez</b>, better known by her <a href="/wiki/Stage_name" title="Stage name">stage name</a> <b>Hurricane G</b>, is an American <a href="/wiki/Rapping" title="Rapping">rapper</a> of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Puerto_Rican_people" title="Puerto Rican people">Puerto Rican</a> descent. Her 1997 single release "Somebody Else" charted at #10 by Billboard Magazine on <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hot_Rap_Singles" title="Hot Rap Singles">Hot Rap Singles</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> and at #54 on their <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hot_R%26B/Hip-Hop_Singles_%26_Tracks" title="Hot R&amp;B/Hip-Hop Singles &amp; Tracks">Hot R&amp;B/Hip-Hop Singles &amp; Tracks</a> chart.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>She was born in <a href="/wiki/Brooklyn" title="Brooklyn">Brooklyn</a>, <a href="/wiki/New_York_(state)" title="New York (state)">New York</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> She was the <a href="/wiki/Hit_Squad" title="Hit Squad">Hit Squad</a>'s first female member, and made guest appearances on albums by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Keith_Murray_(rap)" title="Keith Murray (rap)">Keith Murray</a>, <a href="/wiki/Redman_(rapper)" title="Redman (rapper)">Redman</a>, <a href="/wiki/Xzibit" title="Xzibit">Xzibit</a>, <a href="/wiki/Delinquent_Habits" title="Delinquent Habits">Delinquent Habits</a>, <a href="/wiki/Funkdoobiest" title="Funkdoobiest">Funkdoobiest</a>, the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Cocoa_Brovaz" title="Cocoa Brovaz">Cocoa Brovaz</a>, and others. She also appeared on <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Puff_Daddy" title="Puff Daddy">Puff Daddy</a>'s track "P.E. 2000". Her much-delayed debut <a href="/wiki/Album" title="Album">album</a>, <i><a href="/wiki/All_Woman_(album)" title="All Woman (album)">All Woman</a></i>, was released in 1997.</p>
<p>Hurricane G has a daughter by <a href="/wiki/Erick_Sermon" title="Erick Sermon">Erick Sermon</a> (of <a href="/wiki/EPMD" title="EPMD">EPMD</a> fame) named Lexus.</p>
