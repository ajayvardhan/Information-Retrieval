<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Kalahari High</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div class="noprint" id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
<div id="contentSub"></div>
<div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>The <b>Kalahari High</b> is a semi-permanent <a href="/wiki/Anticyclone" title="Anticyclone">anticyclone</a> situated over the interior of southern Africa. It is part of the <a href="/wiki/Subtropical_ridge" title="Subtropical ridge">subtropical ridge</a> system and the reason the <a href="/wiki/Kalahari_Desert" title="Kalahari Desert">Kalahari</a> is a <a href="/wiki/Desert" title="Desert">desert</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> It is the descending limb of a <a href="/wiki/Hadley_cell" title="Hadley cell">Hadley cell</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
