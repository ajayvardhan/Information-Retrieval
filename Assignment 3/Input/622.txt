<p>The <b>Waterloo Hurricanes</b> were a <a href="/wiki/Junior_ice_hockey" title="Junior ice hockey">junior ice hockey</a> team playing in the <a href="/wiki/Ontario_Hockey_League" title="Ontario Hockey League">Ontario Hockey Association</a> for two seasons between <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/1950%E2%80%9351_OHA_season" title="1950–51 OHA season">1950</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/1951%E2%80%9352_OHA_season" title="1951–52 OHA season">1952</a>. They played at the <a href="/wiki/Waterloo_Memorial_Arena" title="Waterloo Memorial Arena">Waterloo Memorial Arena</a> in <a href="/wiki/Waterloo,_Ontario" title="Waterloo, Ontario">Waterloo, Ontario</a>. The team folded after the 1951–52 season due to financial woes. That season, the Hurricanes competed against cross-town rivals, the <a href="/wiki/Kitchener_Greenshirts" title="Kitchener Greenshirts">Kitchener Greenshirts</a>.</p>
<p>Two alumni of the Hurricanes went on to play in the <a href="/wiki/National_Hockey_League" title="National Hockey League">National Hockey League</a>. Goaltender <a href="/wiki/Norm_Defelice" title="Norm Defelice">Norm Defelice</a> played 10 games with the <a href="/wiki/Boston_Bruins" title="Boston Bruins">Boston Bruins</a> in <a href="/wiki/1956%E2%80%9357_NHL_season" title="1956–57 NHL season">1956–57</a>, and had a long career in the <a href="/wiki/Eastern_Hockey_League" title="Eastern Hockey League">Eastern Hockey League</a>. Defenceman <a href="/wiki/Warren_Godfrey" title="Warren Godfrey">Warren Godfrey</a> played 15 fifteen seasons in the NHL with the Boston Bruins and <a href="/wiki/Detroit_Red_Wings" title="Detroit Red Wings">Detroit Red Wings</a>.</p>
