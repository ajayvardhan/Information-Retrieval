<p>The <i><b>Bulletin of the American Meteorological Society</b></i> is a <a href="/wiki/Scientific_journal" title="Scientific journal">scientific journal</a> published by the <a href="/wiki/American_Meteorological_Society" title="American Meteorological Society">American Meteorological Society</a>. <i>BAMS</i> is the flagship magazine of AMS and publishes <a href="/wiki/Peer_review" title="Peer review">peer reviewed</a> articles of interest and significance for the weather, water, and climate community as well as news, editorials, and reviews for AMS members. <i>BAMS</i> articles are fully open access; AMS members can also access the <a class="external text" href="http://www.ametsoc.org/digitalBams/" rel="nofollow">digital version</a> which replicates the print issue cover-to-cover and often includes enhanced articles with audio and video.</p>
