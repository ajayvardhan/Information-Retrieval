<h3><span id="Expedition_52/53_launch_and_docking"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Expedition_52.2F53_launch_and_docking">Expedition 52/53 launch and docking</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Expedition_53&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Expedition 52/53 launch and docking">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable" role="note">Main article: <a href="/wiki/Soyuz_MS-05" title="Soyuz MS-05">Soyuz MS-05</a></div>
<p><b>Soyuz MS-05</b> launched on July 28, 2017, transporting <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Randy_Bresnik" title="Randy Bresnik">Randy Bresnik</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sergey_Nikolayevich_Ryzhikov" title="Sergey Nikolayevich Ryzhikov">Sergey Ryzhikov</a> and <a href="/wiki/Paolo_Nespoli" title="Paolo Nespoli">Paolo Nespoli</a>, who would make up the Expedition 52/53 crew.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> MS-05 docked with the <a href="/wiki/Rassvet_(ISS_module)" title="Rassvet (ISS module)">Rassvet</a> module six hours later.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span id="Expedition_53/54_launch_and_docking"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Expedition_53.2F54_launch_and_docking">Expedition 53/54 launch and docking</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Expedition_53&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Expedition 53/54 launch and docking">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable" role="note">Main article: <a href="/wiki/Soyuz_MS-06" title="Soyuz MS-06">Soyuz MS-06</a></div>
<p><b>Soyuz MS-06</b> launched on September 13, 2017, transporting <a href="/wiki/Alexander_Misurkin" title="Alexander Misurkin">Alexander Misurkin</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mark_T._Vande_Hei" title="Mark T. Vande Hei">Mark T. Vande Hei</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Joe_Acaba" title="Joe Acaba">Joe Acaba</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> MS-06 docked with the <a href="/wiki/Poisk_(ISS_module)" title="Poisk (ISS module)">(Poisk (MRM-2)</a> module six hours later.</p>
