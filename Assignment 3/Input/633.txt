<p>The <b>Holyoke Hurricanes</b> are a now defunct team of the <a href="/wiki/Independent_Women%27s_Football_League" title="Independent Women's Football League">Independent Women's Football League</a>. Based out of <a href="/wiki/Holyoke,_Massachusetts" title="Holyoke, Massachusetts">Holyoke, Massachusetts</a>, the Hurricanes played their home games at <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Ware_High_School&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Ware High School (page does not exist)">Ware High School</a> in nearby <a href="/wiki/Ware,_Massachusetts" title="Ware, Massachusetts">Ware</a>.</p>
<p>The Hurricanes forfeited the final two games of the 2009 season, and all of the 2010 season.</p>
