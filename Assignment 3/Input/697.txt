<p>A <b>ridge</b> is an elongated region of relatively high <a href="/wiki/Atmospheric_pressure" title="Atmospheric pressure">atmospheric pressure</a>, the opposite of a <a href="/wiki/Trough_(meteorology)" title="Trough (meteorology)">trough</a>.</p>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/Hydrology" title="Hydrology">hydrologic</a> terms, a ridge is a line or wall of broken ice forced up by pressure. May be fresh or weathered.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
