filmography  4 references  5 external links  careeredit  britten is a native of manchester but began writing for the adelaide australia band the twilights a popular 1960s band for which he played lead guitar.4 at times he co-wrote with glenn shorrock and peter Brideoake.5 he also recorded a single under his own name weeks day  britten was a band member of quartet with kevin peek alan tarney and trevor spencer who recorded one album with decca records in the uk which remains unreleasedcitation needed one single was issued in 1969 on decca in the uk and australia and london in the us my lady come UK-F12974,7 aust Y-8977, us london lon 1031 and a second single in the uk only in 1970 where did you fail F13072.  after the twilights broke up he returned to england and moved to london where he did session work brittens multi layered guitars featured on alvin stardusts 1973 hit coo ca choocitation needed in 1973 he was part of cliff richards eurovision song contest 1973 entry and along with john farrar alan tarney and trevor spencer submitted six songs of which to all our friends was chosen and came third after a lean charting period for cliff richard britten gave him woman and in 1976, it became richards first top 10 in the uk for three years his first top 10 hit in the US.8910 he was a guitarist in richards band for many years and was the co-producer and main songwriter for richards 1979 album rock roll juvenile which reached no 3 in the uk album chart he wrote and co-wrote with b a robertson 10 of the 12 songs of which reached no 4 in the uk singles Chart.11  in the early 1980s, brittens psychedelic rock song originally a hit for the twilights was revived by australias divinyls as a b-side to their 1984 single die Young.12  with graham lyle britten also wrote love got to do with it and this became tina turners million selling hit love got to do with it reached no 3 in the uk singles Chart13 and no 1 in the us billboard hot 100, and won britten and lyle the grammy award for song of the year in 1985. it also won the grammy award for record of the year which went to tina Turner.14 later that year they co-wrote dont need another hero for the film mad max beyond thunderdome also sung by tina turner the song reached no 2 in the us and no 3 in the UK.13 britten and lyle received a golden globe award nomination for best original song in 1986. it also earned turner a 1986 grammy nomination for best female pop vocal performance.15 he also acted as a record producer for Turner.16  britten co-wrote good friends for michael jacksons bad album.17 britten has also penned songs for olivia newton-john including make me strong and up with graham lyle he has also written for meat loaf melissa manchester bonnie raitt and hank Marvin.20 britten continues to compose from his home base in rural england but has returned to australia on occasion including the twilights reunion for the long way to the top concert tour.5  in 2002, the song tax inevitable co-written by britten and charlie dore appeared on celine dions album a new day has Come.21  britten presently has a home in richmond london and a home recording studio called of the Ark.22
