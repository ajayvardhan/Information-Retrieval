university corporation for atmospheric research  the university corporation for atmospheric research is a nonprofit consortium of more than 100 colleges and universities providing research and training in the atmospheric and related sciences.1 ucar manages the national center for atmospheric research and provides additional services to strengthen and support research and education through its community programs its headquarters in boulder colorado include ncars mesa laboratory designed by im pei  ucar was established in 1959 by faculty from 14 leading universities to support and nourish the atmospheric sciences they were motivated by a newly recognized need for pooled observational and computational facilities and a strong research staff which together would allow the academic community to carry out complex long-term scientific programs beyond the reach of individual universities.2  this groups first major action in partnership with the national science foundation was to establish ncar since then ucar has managed ncar on behalf of nsf to address pressing scientific and societal needs involving the atmosphere and its interactions with the oceans land and sunwhat
