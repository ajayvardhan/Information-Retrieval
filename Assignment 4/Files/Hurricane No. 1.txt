drummer gareth farmer after releasing two albums hurricane and only the strongest will survive the band broke-up bell went to join gay dad and then eventually oasis while lowe ventured into a solo career fronted by lowe the band reformed in 2014 and released their third album find what you love and let it kill you in november 2015.  contents  1 history  1.1 formation hurricane no 1 and only the
