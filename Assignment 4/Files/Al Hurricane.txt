22, 2017, known professionally as al hurricane was an american singer-songwriter dubbed godfather of new mexico music.23 he released more than thirty albums and is best known for his contributions to new mexicos unique style of spanish music.4  he received his nickname hurricane from his mother as a child he would accidentally knock things over5 the nickname became synonymous with his band studio and a recording label.6 his signature look which included an eye-patch was due to an automobile accident that occurred during the 1960s.7 these things as well as his performance style led to hurricane being known for his ability to improvise and adapt.8 they have also led to his music and image being ultimately entangled with the history of new mexico music.9  al hurricane was often accompanied on stage by his children sons al hurricane jr and jerry dean and daughter erika he also performed frequently with his brothers baby gaby and tiny morrie he was the subject of a tribute concert which was
