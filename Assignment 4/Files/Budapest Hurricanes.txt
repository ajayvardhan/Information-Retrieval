budapest hurricanes were an american football team based in budapest hungary the team competed in the hungarian football league and the ifaf cei cup the hurricanes were founded in 2009 and have become a dominant team of hungary since although the team played only three friendly games in 2010, since 2011 they were a full member of the domestic american football competitions after the hurricanes were the champions of the hfl and the youth cup as well in 2013, the team had been dissolved in 2014.  contents  1 seasons  1.1 2010  1.2 2011  1.3 2012  1.4 2013  1.5 2014  2 players  2.1 current roster
