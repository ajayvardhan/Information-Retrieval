erwin galang  tropical depression is a filipino reggae band formed in 1990 by vocalistguitarist dominic gamboa and guitarist lawrence lennon eudela aside from gamboa and eudela the bands classic line-up was composed of guitarist lito crisostomo bassist pex holigores keyboardists tj espinola and mike llacar back-up vocalists arlene empalmado and teng santaromana-gamboa and drummer percussionist and back-up vocalist lala lejeune  the band was one of the several bands who spearheaded the 1990s pinoy rock explosion and popularized the songs kapayapaan bilog na naman ang buwan and alaala  hiatusedit  the group went into hiatus in 2013 following the death of original lead vocalist papadom gamboa gamboa died a day before his 48th birthday in december 2013. the wake of his burial
