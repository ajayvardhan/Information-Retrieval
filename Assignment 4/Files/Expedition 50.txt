began upon the departure of soyuz MS-01 on october 28,1 2016 and was concluded upon the departure of soyuz MS-02 on april 10, 2017. the crew of soyuz MS-03 were transferred to expedition 51.  after the launch of soyuz MS-03, peggy whitson at age 56, became the oldest woman to fly into space by taking command of expedition 51 on april 10, 2017, she also became the first woman to command two iss
