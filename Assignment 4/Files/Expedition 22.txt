expedition 21  expedition 23  expedition 22 was the 22nd long duration crew flight to the international space station this expedition began in november 2009 when the expedition 21 crew departed for a period of 3 weeks there were only 2 crew members it was the first time that had happened since STS-114. commander jeff williams and flight engineer maksim surayev were joined by the rest of their crew on 22 december 2009, making the expedition 22 a crew of five.1  the expedition ended when soyuz TMA-16 undocked on 17 march 2010, and was immediately followed by the start of expedition 23.  contents
