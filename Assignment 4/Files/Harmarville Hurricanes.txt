harmarville pennsylvania  the harmarville hurricanes soccer club were an amateur soccer club from harmarville pennsylvania a suburb of pittsburgh that twice won the lamar hunt us open cup known then as the national challenge cup in 1952 and 1956. they also played in the cup final in 1953. the 1956 final saw the hurricanes win 32 on aggregate score after a comeback from a two-goal deficit they
