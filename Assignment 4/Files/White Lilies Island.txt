performanceedit  in australia the album debuted at number 3 and was certified gold despite enjoying only a small resurgence in sales with subsequent singles the album debuted at number 15 on the uk albums chart with 21,000 in sales before dropping out of the top 75 in its fourth week it re-entered the top 30 with the release of the second single impression and sold eventually nearly 200,000 copies in the uk overall in the us the album debuted at number 35 with 35,000 in sales and dropped out of the top 200 after seven weeks eventually selling 215,000 overallcitation needed worldwide
